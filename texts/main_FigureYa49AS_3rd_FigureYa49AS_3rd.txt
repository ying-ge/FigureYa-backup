In the most popular third-generation sequencing nowadays, alternative splicing is definitely an aspect that needs to be analyzed. The sashimi plot can be obtained through IGV to quantitatively display the situation of alternative splicing.

处理流程
process flow
UniTransModel
Cogent分析见
三代转录组系列：使用Cogent重建基因组编码区
Cogent analysis sees
the three-generation transcriptome series: using Cogent to reconstruct the genome coding area
二：FASTA格式的转录亚型(isoform)。 使用GMAP/Minimap2将纠错后非冗余的转录本(也就是上面cd-hist-est处理后，Cogent处理前的的序列)回帖到UniTransModels，之后合并具有相同剪切位点(splicing junctions)，同一个UniTransModels下具有不同剪切位点的转录本就被认为是转录亚型(transcription isoforms)。
冗余isofroms合并见
Tutorial: Collapse redundant isoforms without genome
2: Transcription isoforms in FASTA format. Using GMAP/Minimap2, the corrected and non-redundant transcripts (i.e., the sequences obtained after the cd-hist-est processing and before the Cogent processing) are mapped back to UniTransModels. Then, those with the same splicing junctions are merged. Transcripts with different splicing junctions within the same UniTransModels are regarded as transcription isoforms.
For redundant isoform merging, please refer to
Tutorial: Collapse redundant isoforms without genome
三：二代测序数据，用于对不同剪切事件进行定量。二代数据由于内容过大，请按照如下方法下载
