需求描述
Requirement description
TCGA里的正常组织太少，把GTEx的正常组织加进去，用分裂小提琴图展示某个基因在TCGA肿瘤和正常组织中的表达差异。
There is too little normal tissue in TCGA, add normal tissue from
GTEx and use split violin plots to show the difference in expression of
a gene in TCGA tumor and normal tissue.
出自
https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0896627318308481
,
Supplemental material, Figure 3b
from
https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0896627318308481
,
Supplemental material, Figure 3b
应用场景
Application scenario
适用于：
对比某一基因在TCGA肿瘤组织和正常组织(TCGA的normal和GTEx)基因表达量TPM值
用分裂小提琴图展示表达量数据
Applicable to:
compare gene expression TPM values for a gene in TCGA tumor
tissue and normal tissue (TCGA’s normal and GTEx)
Display expression data using split violin plots
