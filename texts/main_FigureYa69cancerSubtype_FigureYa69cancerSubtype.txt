友情科普：
共识聚类的原理是通过重抽样的思想，对原始数据集做双维度的重抽样，在扰动子集的基础上反复聚类
多次聚类结果累加求平均得到聚类共识，再在共识矩阵的基础上再聚类得最终聚类结果。
Friendship clustering:
The principle of consensus clustering is through the idea of
resampling, the original dataset to do two-dimensional resampling, in
the perturbation of the subset of the basis of repeated clustering
Multiple clustering results are accumulated and averaged to obtain
the clustering consensus, and then clustered again on the basis of the
consensus matrix to get the final clustering results.

方法 3.ExecuteCNMF
该方法是层次聚类和非负矩阵分解的结合
注意：
NMF不允许出现负值，但是在CancerSubtype包的处理下，负值会利用Kim
& Tidor’s trick 法来处理
这个trick会将所有负值拉至0，所有正值扩大两倍，简单来说就是自己加上自己。
NMF的方法有许多的数学要求，对于稀疏矩阵（0较多的矩阵），往往很难进行分解而报error，所以突变数据，甚至突变签名数据请尽量避免出现在多组学聚类中，可换用离散CNA数据，或甲基化数据
此外，大n大p情况运行共识非负矩阵分解，请保持足够的耐心…建议先进行降维操作
Method 3.ExecuteCNMF
This method is a combination of hierarchical clustering and
non-negative matrix factorization
Note:
NMF does not allow negative values, but under
the CancerSubtype package, negative values are handled by Kim &
Tidor’s trick.
This trick pulls all negative values to 0 and doubles all positive
values, simply adding themselves.
NMF method has a lot of mathematical requirements, for sparse
matrices (more 0’s), it is often difficult to decompose and report
error, so mutant data, even mutant signature data, please try to avoid
appearing in the multi-homology clustering, can be replaced with
discrete CNA data, or methylation data!
In addition, please be patient when running consensus non-negative
matrix decomposition for large n large p cases… It is recommended to run
the dimensionality reduction operation first
